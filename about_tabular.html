About Me

Originally from the East Coast, I've lived in Metro DC, Manhttan, Los Angeles, Phoenix, Seattle, Boulder, Denver; and Florence, Italy. In 2014 I finished an Associate of Science of Business Management and Leadership with a concentration in Entrepreneurship. That same year, my spectacular daughter was born.

I love what I do for work. I don't get most jokes. You can find me snowboarding every free weekend day that I have. Often I just get the urge to travel so I go hop on a last minute flight somewhere. I don't really play games, although I got into Steep recently. I grew up playing Baldur's Gate. My childhood home was a pink-brick, French-rooved haven with seven gardens in the yard and the Catoctin Creek flowing through the Blue Ridge Mountains out back. Those summer nights sleeping down in the woods, with olfactory senses engrossed in the scents of grasses and insects; some nights darker than our eyes can define, some nights the sky shone brighter than would a million lightning bugs above me. And that, is why I write poetry.
